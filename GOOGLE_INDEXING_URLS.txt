========================================
HOW TO SUBMIT TO GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE
========================================

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console

2. Verify your website ownership

3. Submit Sitemap:
   - Click "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: sitemap.xml
   - Click "Submit"

4. Request Indexing for Individual URLs:
   - Click "URL Inspection" at top
   - Paste each URL above
   - Click "Request Indexing"

5. Priority Order (submit in this order):
   - Home (/)
   - About
   - Features
   - Contact
   - FAQ
   - Support
   - Employee Login
   - Privacy Policy
   - Terms and Conditions


========================================
IMPORTANT NOTES
========================================

- Protected routes (/admin, /employee/dashboard, etc.) are NOT included
- Only public pages should be indexed
- Sitemap is automatically updated with all public URLs
- robots.txt blocks admin and employee protected routes
- Wait 24-48 hours for Google to crawl after submission
- Check indexing status in Google Search Console
